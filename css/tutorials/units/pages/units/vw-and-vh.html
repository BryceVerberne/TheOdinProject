For every unit of vw or vh, that is a percentage of the screen that you are taking up. Therefore, 50vw will take up 50% of the width screen and 50vh will take up 50% of the height of the screen.

How is this any different from percentages? Well, percentages are relative to their parent. So, if you have a parent box that has a width of 100px, and you have a box inside this parent that is assigned a width of 50%, it will take up 50px of the parent box. On the other hand, vw and vh do not take into consideration their parents. Therefore, if we replaced 50% will 50vw, it would take up 50% of the entire screen width and ignore the parent box of 100px.
