Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers

Description

Grilled Teriyaki Shrimp and Pineapple Skewers are a delightful blend of succulent shrimp and sweet pineapple, enhanced by a homemade teriyaki sauce. This sauce, featuring soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger, balances sweet, salty, and umami flavors, creating a unique, tasty marinade for the shrimp. Finished with sesame seeds and green onions, the dish boasts additional crunch and a vibrant color palette.

In this recipe, the shrimp, marinated in the teriyaki sauce and then grilled, is a flavor-packed star. The addition of grilled pineapple provides a sweet, slightly charred counterpoint to the savory shrimp. Served on skewers, this pairing becomes an easy-to-handle, festive dish â€“ a delightful culinary creation merging simple ingredients with rich flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh garlic
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 pound jumbo shrimp
  • 1/2 fresh pineapple, cored and cut into 1 1/2-inch pieces
  • skewers
  • 2 tablespoons minced fresh cilantro (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds (Optional)

Directions

  1. Combine water, soy sauce, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and ginger in a small saucepan and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until sauce has reduced and thickened slightly, 8 to 10 minutes.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil grate. Thread shrimp and pineapple alternately onto skewers and place on a platter.
  3. Arrange skewers on the hot grate. Grill 2 to 3 minutes per side, or until shrimp is opaque and cooked through. Turn grill to low heat and brush sauce on both sides of skewers. Transfer to a serving platter and sprinkle with cilantro and sesame seeds.
